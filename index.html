MOTIVATION LETTER

Through my profession I define my prime objective to design, develop and support effective and well-laid data- and information management solutions. Nowadays the volume of information available can be regarded almost incomprehensible, also the pace of evolution in information technology is faster than ever. These factors pose challenges for all organizations, yet the collection, maintenance, and the presentation of data for decision making a cost-effective and sustainable way can mean such a competitive edge what must be considered by all entities operating under market conditions. Exploiting any advanced capability on solid foundations is a key for success. In my opinion and per my experience leveraging on data assets is the key for proper decision making and any other use-case. Therefore, it must be ensured the appropriate consumers have the adequate information at the right time. For me personally it means I should be interested in almost any kind of software, tool, solution, methodology or concept that can support the achievement of this goal. Through my career I have participated with success in many projects and initiatives meeting the above objective. I had the chance to work with different technologies across many environments filling different roles over time. I strongly believe with my business and IT knowledge, skills, also blending those of course, I can efficiently support the data management problems of any business area, contributing to the success of a domain or an organisation.
